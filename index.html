Models

Character Models

Tsumiyo, Spirit Witch

This latest model is brewing up some mystical magic! Based on the artist Alexis Pflaum character, I created this model with great enthusiasm. Using tools such as Maya, Blender, and Substance Painter, I conjured every curve, color, and edge, with precision and passion. Together we can bring more character to life through 3D models.

Wife

This 3D was created for the short film Winters Remedy, a heart-wrench short film that garnered attention from various film festivals across the United States. She proved a challenge to present a sickly and frail character while also being believable. Joining a group of talented artists on this project was a privilege and remarkable experience. I hope to collaborate with other artists in the future to bring a new vision to life.

Andromeda

This model began with a sketch created by an artist, Lola Zhang. In a whirlwind of inspiration I sculpted this model using Maya, Blender, Substance Painter, and Photoshop. This adventure of bringing this sketch into a 3D realm was both challenging and rewarding.

Prop Models

Rosa Gun

This gun was inspired from the Bayonetta game. I used maya and substance painter to create this weapon. A beautiful sleek design with small detail was both fun to create and provided new perspectives to beautiful weapon designs.

Guitar

The deep red of Halestorm guitar has always caught my eyes. This 3D model was created based off of her iconic guitar. Using tools such as maya, substance painter, and photoshop to create her guitar in a 3D environment.

Garden Globe

This garden globe is a 3D prop model that I made to showcase my ability to work with realism. I used maya, substance painter, and photoshop to create this model.

Zelda

Waiting each day for the new zelda game was both exciting and nerve racking as a long time fan. To pass the time I created a 3D model based off of what the design of the case could have looked like. I created Nintendos Iconic case using Maya, photoshop, and substance painter. My personal goal for this piece was to trick people into thinking I got the game early. (Which worked surprisingly well.) This 3D model looks deceptively easy to create, but proved to be one of the harder models I have created.
